About

I’m Arthur (Chi-Wei Lee), double majoring in Physics and the Interdisciplinary Program of EECS (AI Track) at National Tsing Hua University. My research interests focus on Computer Vision, Machine Learning, and Generative AI. I am currently involved in diffusion-based dynamic QR code generation at Academia Sinica CITI (aesthetic optimization + scan robustness).

I’m good at turning “research ideas → runnable systems → demo-ready results” quickly: I’ve led an anomaly-detection model integration project and was invited to present at an AAAI Workshop, and I’ve also built hardware–machine learning integrations (Arduino) to demonstrate an automated diagnostic device.
